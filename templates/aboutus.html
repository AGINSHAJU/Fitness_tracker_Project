About Us

Welcome! We’re excited to bring you a dual-purpose platform that combines sustainability and personal health into one powerful tool.

Carbon Tracker helps you monitor and reduce your carbon footprint by tracking travel, energy use, and diet. With interactive maps, charts, points, and AI-driven tips, we make eco-friendly living rewarding and fun.

Fitness Tracker empowers you to take charge of your health by logging weight, waist size, exercise, and calories. See your progress with charts, get health warnings, and earn badges as you work toward your fitness goals.

Why It Matters Whether it’s saving the planet or improving your well-being, every step counts. We’re here to provide the insights and motivation you need to make a difference in both areas.

Who We Are A team passionate about technology, sustainability, and fitness, we’ve built this platform using Django to support your journey—whether you’re an eco-warrior, a fitness enthusiast, or both!

Join Us! Start tracking today and see how your actions shape a healthier you and a greener world.
